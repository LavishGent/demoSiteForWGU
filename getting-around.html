Getting Around

How to reach Taniti and move around the island once you arrive.

Arriving

Most visitors fly into Taniti's small airport, which currently serves small jets and propeller planes — an expansion is underway to accommodate larger aircraft. A cruise ship also docks at Yellow Leaf Bay once a week for a one-night stay.

From the airport

Taxis are available in Taniti City and can take you from the airport to your lodging. Prefer to drive yourself? There is a rental car agency near the airport, so you can pick up a car as soon as you land.

Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily. Outside the city, privately owned buses serve the rest of the island.

Bikes & walking

Bikes are available to rent — note that Tanitian law requires you to wear a helmet while riding. Taniti City itself is flat and easy to explore on foot, and Merriton Landing on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay is walkable too.
