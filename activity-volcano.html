Volcano Tour

$65 per person (demonstration pricing)

≈ 4 hours, bus pickup from Taniti City

What to expect

Join a guided tour of Taniti's small active volcano, deep in the island's mountainous interior. Your guide leads the group to safe viewpoints overlooking the crater and shares the volcano's place in Tanitian life. A tour bus picks you up in Taniti City and returns you the same day — no rental car needed.

Book this activity

Getting there: pickup is in Taniti City — see Getting Around for buses, taxis, and rental cars.
