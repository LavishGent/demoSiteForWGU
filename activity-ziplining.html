Zip-lining

$89 per person (demonstration pricing)

≈ 3 hours, Merriton Landing departure

What to expect

Glide through Taniti's rainforest canopy on a guided zip-line course. Your guides handle the setup at every platform, and standard zip-line safety gear — harness, helmet, and gloves — is provided and required for every rider. Between lines you'll walk short rainforest trails, so wear closed-toe shoes. The tour departs from Merriton Landing on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Book this activity

Getting there: Merriton Landing is reachable by bus, taxi, bike, or on foot — see Getting Around.
