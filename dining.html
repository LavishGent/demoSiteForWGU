Dining

Taniti has ten restaurants: five serving local fish and rice dishes, three American-style, and two Pan-Asian.

Local — fish & rice (5)

Yellow Leaf Kitchen

Local fish and rice dishes near the bay.

Reef & Rice

The day's catch served with traditional Tanitian rice.

Tanitian Table

Home-style local fish and rice plates.

Bayfront Grill

Grilled local fish with rice, steps from the water.

Island Hearth

Local fish and rice classics in Taniti City.

American (3)

Landing Diner

American-style comfort food at Merriton Landing.

Harbor Burger Co.

Burgers and fries — familiar American fare.

Stateside Cafe

American breakfasts and lunches in Taniti City.

Pan-Asian (2)

Lotus Bay

Pan-Asian dishes with a Tanitian twist.

Monsoon Noodle House

Noodles, rice bowls, and Pan-Asian favorites.

Groceries

Prefer to cook? Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one 24-hour convenience store.
